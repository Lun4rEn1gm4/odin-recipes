Felix Felicis - Potion

Overview

Felix Felicis, also known as Liquid Luck, is a shimmering golden potion that makes the drinker lucky for a period of time, during which everything they attempt will be successful. It is a tricky potion to make and dangerous if consumed in large quantities, leading to recklessness and overconfidence.

Ingredients

  • 4 drops of Morning Dew (collected before sunrise)
  • 2 crushed Occamy eggshells
  • 1 pinch of powdered Moonstone
  • 3 Crushed Gurdyroot leaves
  • 1 golden snidget feather (symbolic)
  • 2 teaspoons of Salamander blood

Brewing Instructions

  1. Pour the morning dew and moonstone powder into a brass cauldron.
  2. Heat gently over a silver flame until lightly simmering.
  3. Stir clockwise three times while whispering "Fortuna major."
  4. Add Gurdyroot and crushed Occamy shell; continue stirring until the mixture gleams.
  5. Slowly pour in salamander blood — the potion should fizz and glow gold.
  6. Drop in the feather and let the mixture brew for six lunar rotations.
  7. Bottle carefully. Only a few drops are needed — overuse may result in uncontrollable energy and chaotic optimism.
