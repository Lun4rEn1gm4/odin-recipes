Amortentia - Potion

Overview

Amortentia is the most powerful love potion in existence. It causes a powerful infatuation or obsession in the drinker. It has a distinctive smell that varies according to what each individual finds most attractive. The potion emits steam in characteristic spirals and glows with a pearlescent sheen.

Ingredients

  • 5 rose petals soaked in morning dew
  • 3 crushed pearls
  • 1 vanilla bean (split)
  • 1 strand of unicorn tail hair
  • 2 drops of essence of violet
  • (Optional) Steam from a boiling romantic poem

Brewing Instructions

  1. Begin by infusing the rose petals in hot water until fragrant and soft.
  2. Add crushed pearls and stir until the mixture shimmers.
  3. Drop in the unicorn hair — the potion should swirl in spiral patterns on its own.
  4. Split and scrape the vanilla bean into the potion, stirring gently counter-clockwise.
  5. Drip in essence of violet and breathe deeply — the smell will reflect your heart’s true desire.
  6. Optional: Let the potion simmer while reciting or playing a romantic poem to enhance the potency.
  7. Do not use to manipulate free will. Handle with care — and heart.
